The Apothecary Diaries - Season 1

Season Overview

Season 1 introduces us to Maomao, a brilliant young apothecary who finds herself working in the imperial palace after being kidnapped and sold as a servant. Despite her lowly position, her extensive knowledge of medicine, poisons, and herbs makes her an invaluable asset in solving the palace's many mysteries.
